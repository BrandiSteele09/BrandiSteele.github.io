BREAKING NEWS

Today, it has been reported that a recent shopping mall has opened
in Lancaster County. The shopping mall has been in progess of
being built for the past year and a half, and has been deemed the
Largest Mall in Pennsylvania. Nearby citizens are
ecstatic that they finally have a mall within the area that is close.
The mall has been named Superstar Mall and is located
next to Lancaster County Diner. The mall is already loaded with
people.

Superstar mall is rather large, and has been built with 6 different
wings of attractions, stores, food courts, and many other things. There's
a bowling alley, a racing track, and virtual reality simulators. There's
also laser tag, a golf course, and a huge stage where animatronics can
dance and perform. The stores include anything from jewelry, to clothes,
to even smart phones! Hundreds of kids pile between rides, games, and stores
within the building, and there's a lot of staff to help maintain crowds.

Many citizens and guests that have visited the mall said that it
is extremely clean and organized. Some reported that the rides are
extremely safe and kid friendly. The animatronics are polished and
designed well to entertain both children and parents alike.
We love this mall! We are so happy
that we have a mall close by and no longer have to travel hours
and hours to find a good one.

- A local mother that had just recently visited the establishment.
