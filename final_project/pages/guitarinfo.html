So, the ultimate question. What is a guitar?

Well, a guitar is a musical instrument that takes time to learn, much like any other instrument. There are many parts to a guitar. These parts include:

  • The headstock and tuner pegs
  • The neck and fingerboards

    • (These parts are the upper half of the guitar!)

  • The body
  • >The strings
    • These are what makes the sounds!
      • (They are plucked, which cause them to vibrate and create pretty sounds!)

    (These parts are the lower half of the guitar!)
