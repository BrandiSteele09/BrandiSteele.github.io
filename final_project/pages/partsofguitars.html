There are many, MANY different parts of guitars, and all of them together create a beautiful instrument!

Starting off as the top of the guitar, we have the headstock and the tuners/pegs!

This is how the guitar is tuned. You turn the pegs up or down and they make the strings tighter (sharper) or looser (flat).

Then, We have the neck, the frets, and the strings. These are where you play notes and where the guitar makes sound.

Finally, we have the body of the guitar. These can have many different shapes and sizes, much like the headstock. This, alongside the strings, is what vibrates and making sound.
